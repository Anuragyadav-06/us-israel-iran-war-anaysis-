# us-israel-iran-war-anaysis-
# Investor Behavior During the 2026 US-Israel-Iran War 📊

## What This Project Analyzes
How Gold, Oil, S&P 500, Defense Stocks and Fear Index 
moved across 3 phases of the 2026 Iran War

## 5 Key Findings
- Gold peaked BEFORE war started — Buy Rumor Sell News
- Oil surged +24.5% — the only real war trade
- Fear (VIX) correlated -0.71 with stocks, not gold
- S&P 500 most volatile DURING ceasefire, not war
- Defense stocks barely moved — war was too short

## Tools Used

## Data Range
January 1, 2026 — May 30, 2026
